Short Bio: Kristal is a mom of 4 and a company owner in Charlotte, North Carolina.


If you’re looking for a REALTOR® with passion, enthusiasm and deep expertise, you’ve come to the right place.

As a digital native with a marketing background, I create highly strategic and engaging marketing campaigns for each and every one of my clients, to make sure their homes get top dollar


A word of advice: Make sure that you have a clear and realistic objective when it comes to your business and family and makes sure you work towards getting it done. The goals will help you fulfill and crush those goals as you grow as a family as well as in your business. Be sure to include what you're supposed to do together. Money comes and goes, but our family's emotional and psychological needs should be part of our daily routine and goals. Most often you will find yourself worn out. Giving your all to your family shows your dedication, but honestly: who will take care of you? Especially if you're the glue holding everything together, doing too much could break and weaken its strength. It's important to find time to recharge and take care of your own needs. With discipline, an open mindset, and determination, it's possible to balance both work and family successfully and have things work out in your favor. Never give up, you will always be your own biggest supporter.
